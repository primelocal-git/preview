📈 Conversion Tracking

How we measure results

We installed tracking so every lead that comes through this campaign is measured and attributed correctly. This data tells us exactly which keywords, ads, and cities are driving calls and form fills.

📞
Call Tracking
Dynamic number swap on your website tracks which calls come from Google Ads. Each call is logged with the keyword and ad that triggered it.
📝
Form Submit Tracking
When a visitor submits a contact form on your site, the conversion is tracked back to the specific ad click that brought them in.

Conversion data typically takes 24–48 hours to appear in reports. Once we have enough conversion data (usually 2–4 weeks), we can switch to automated bidding that optimizes for leads rather than clicks.
